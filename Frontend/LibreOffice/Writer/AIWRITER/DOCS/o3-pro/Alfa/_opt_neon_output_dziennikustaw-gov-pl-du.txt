- The main page of the website provides access to the Dziennik Ustaw (Journal of Laws) and Monitor Polski.
- There are navigation options including Home, Content, Journal of Laws, Monitor Polski, Search, List of Certificates, Contact, and Help.
- The website is managed by the Government Legislation Centre (RzÄ…dowe Centrum Legislacji, RCL).
- Users can search for legal acts by year, position, or title.
- According to the Act of July 20, 2000, on the promulgation of normative acts and certain other legal acts (Journal of Laws of 2019, item 1461), the Dziennik Ustaw of the Republic of Poland is issued by the Prime Minister with the assistance of the Government Legislation Centre.
- Since January 1, 2012, the Dziennik Ustaw has been published in electronic form, maintaining the order of items within each calendar year.
- The date of promulgation of a legal act is the date it is published electronically on the website of the issuing authority.
- The date of promulgation in electronic form is included in the header of the page.
- The website provides a list of legal acts published in the Dziennik Ustaw of the Republic of Poland.
