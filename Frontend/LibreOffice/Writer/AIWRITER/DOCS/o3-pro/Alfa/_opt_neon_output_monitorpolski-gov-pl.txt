- The homepage provides access to the Monitor Polski, which is the Official Gazette of the Republic of Poland.
- The site includes navigation options such as "Go to content," "Monitor Polski," "Dziennik Ustaw," "Monitor Polski," "Search," "List of certificates," "Contact," and "Help."
- There is a section for "Official Journals."
- The website is operated by the Government Legislation Centre (Rządowe Centrum Legislacji), as indicated by the RCL logo.
- Users can navigate by year and position number to find specific documents.
- There is a search function that allows users to search by title.
- According to the Act of July 20, 2000, on the promulgation of normative acts and certain other legal acts (Journal of Laws of 2019, item 1461), the Official Gazette of the Republic of Poland "Monitor Polski" is issued by the Prime Minister with the assistance of the Government Legislation Centre.
- Since January 1, 2012, Monitor Polski has been published in electronic form, maintaining the order of items within each calendar year.
- The date of promulgation of a legal act is the date it is published electronically on the website of the issuing authority.
- The date of promulgation in electronic form is included in the header of the page.
- The site provides a list of legal acts published in Monitor Polski.
- The most recently published legal acts are listed with their position number, title, date of publication, and a link to download the document in PDF format.
